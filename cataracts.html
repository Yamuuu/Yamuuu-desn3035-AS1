Cataracts

This section of the website is for learning about cataracts.

What are Cataracts?

Cataracts are a common eye condition where the lens of the eye becomes cloudy, leading to decreased vision.
The lens is normally clear, allowing light to pass through and focus on the retina.

What are the causes of Cataracts?

  • Aging
  • Injury
  • Medications
  • Other medical conditions

Symptoms

  • Faded/yellowed colors
  • Clouded/blurred vision
  • Seeing light "halos"
  • Light sensitivity
  • Double vision
